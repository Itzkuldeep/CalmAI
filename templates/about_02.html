About calmAI

Your Partner in Mental Well-being

Our Mission

At calmAI, our mission is to provide accessible mental health support through innovative AI technology. We aim to create a safe, supportive, and understanding environment for everyone.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health resources are easily accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere. Through our AI-powered tools, we strive to make mental health support more personalized and effective.

Our Story

calmAI was founded by a team of passionate individuals who recognized the growing need for mental health support in today's fast-paced world. With backgrounds in psychology, AI technology, and wellness, we came together to create a platform that can truly make a difference.

Meet the Team

Our team consists of experts in psychology, artificial intelligence, and wellness, all dedicated to improving mental health care. We believe in the power of collaboration and innovation to bring the best support to our users.

What We Offer

  • 24/7 AI Chat Support
  • Mental Health Resources
  • Personalized Self-Assessment
  • Wellness Activities and Tips
